The Problem

This research has two key dimensions:
  • Online Social and Knowledge Markets. We examine online social media platforms through economic lens, modeling them as markets. We primarily focus on community question answering (CQA) websites, which we model as knowledge markets; however, our research has broader implications for other types of online social media including online social networks (OSNs), which can be modeled as social markets.
  • Sustainability, Stability and Resilience. We investigate why some online social media succeed, while many others fail. In this regard, we study the underlying social and knowledge markets to reveal the foundations of success or failure.
