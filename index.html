Sustainability of Social Media Knowledge Markets

About

This research has two key dimensions:
  • Social Media Knowledge Markets. We examine online social media platforms through economic lens, modeling them as markets. While we primarily focus on community question answering (CQA) websites, our research has broader implications for other types of online social media including online social networks (OSNs).
  • Sustainability, Stability and Resilience. We investigate why some online social media succeed, while many others fail. In this regard, we study underlying market characteristics to reveal the foundations of success or failure.

Papers

  • Himel Dev, Chase Geigle, Qingtao Hu, Jiahui Zheng, and Hari Sundaram. Size Does Matter: Why Knowledge Markets Can Fail at Scale!. arXiv: 1712.00139.
